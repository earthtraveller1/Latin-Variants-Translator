What is Pig Latin?


Pig Latin is a part of the joke latin languages, in which every word ends with (first letter of a word)ay.

For example, Hello would be ellohay (or ello-hay). the h at the beginning got moved to the end, then add an ay.

One exception is when a word starts with a vowel, the first letter doesn't move, and instead of ending with ay, the word ends with yay. For example, Asterisk would be Asteriskyay.
